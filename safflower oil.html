Safflower Oil Constituents

Safflower seeds consist of 38%–48% oil, which has 6%–8% palmitic (C16:0), 2%–3% stearic (C18:0), 16%–20% oleic (C18:1), 0.2%–0.4% linolenic (C18:3), and 71%–75% linoleic acids (C18:2) in its composition
