Cold Pressed Oil

Cold-pressed oil, also called cold-drawn, or virgin, oil, is purer and has a better flavor than refined oil

    Cold pressing is a oldest way to make oils in Indian civilization since.

    We are using the technically best wooden equipment to extract the Virgin Oil from Ground Nut, Sesame seeds, Sunflower Seeds, Coconut Pods.

  • It involves crushing nuts/seeds into a paste, then applying force with a mechanical press to separate the oil from the pulp.
  • Wooden Extractor is used for extracting the oil
  • Temperature kept below 50°C during extraction

After extraction the oil is filtered, no any chemical treatment is carried out to remove any solid particals. The solid particals are removed by sedimentation process. The clear oil is decanded and packed.

Benefits of Cold Pressed oil

Cold pressed oil is in the most pure form.

  • Oil with it's nutritional value
  • Chemical free
  • No denaturation of oil constituents
  • It has high energy
  • Aroma, flaour is intact
  • All medicinal value is intact

Nutritional Value

  • High content of unsaturated oil
  • linoleic acid (41% of total), oleic acid (39%), palmitic acid (8%), stearic acid (5%)
  • High calorie oil
  • Flaour is intact

Medicinal Value

Constituent depend upon varieties: Sesame oil is composed of the following fatty acids: linoleic acid (41% of total), oleic acid (39%), palmitic acid (8%), stearic acid (5%) and others in small amounts.

  • Having lower LDL (Bad Cholesterol)
